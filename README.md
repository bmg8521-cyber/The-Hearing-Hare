## ğŸµ How It Works

### Mood Recommendations System
When you share your mood:
1. Select from 10 mood options (Sad, Anxious, Happy, Angry, Lonely, Excited, Confused, Peaceful, Stressed, Hopeful)
2. Optionally add more details about your feelings
3. Receive 2-3 curated song recommendations with:
   - Song title and artist
   - Why this music might help
   - Mental health tips specific to your mood
   - Professional resource reminders

### Forum System
- **Browse by Type**: Recent discussions, genre-specific, or trending topics
- **Clickable Posts**: Each forum post opens to a detailed discussion page
- **Reply to Comments**: Build threaded conversations with nested replies
- **Like System**: Show support with likes on posts and comments
- **Create New Forums**: Share your own music and mental health experiences
